About this project

The main purpose is to give people the opportunity to learn some things about sailing. We (Odhran and Tiernan) love to sail and we would like others to try it out too.

Most of this project was written in HTML5 using Bootstrap CSS to give some layout to the pages. Each lesson is a seperate HTML page, so that people can restart where they stopped. The answers to the quizs are checked with Javascript.

Ahoy there: Beginners Introduction to Sailing

This gives some lessons to teach you a few tips when you start sailing. There are also some quizs, but don't worry if you get it wrong.

Are you ready?

Lesson 1: Learn the various parts of a boat and what they are used for.

9 mins

Lesson 2: A second lesson on what to wear before going on the water.

5 mins

Lesson 3: Third lesson covering Jetties and Slips.

3 mins

Lesson 4: Buoys - covering the types of objects you will encounter on water.

3 mins

Lesson 5: This will explain the impact weather has on sailing, as well as explaining the various techniques used when sailing.

9 mins

Lesson 6: Where and how to "park" / moor your boat in a safe way.

9 mins

Lesson 7: This lesson covers looking after your boats and explains the meaning of rigging and de-rigging.

9 mins

Lesson 8: This lesson covers the various types of sailing boats. It also covers some powerboats.

10 mins

This is the end of the lessons. Now all you go to do is turn up and sail.
