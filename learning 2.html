What to wear?

This time I will tell what to wear going sailing.

Layers

The best advice is to wear layers of clothing, as you can get cold if you fall into the water.

  1. The first thing is your swim suit and a rash vest. These keep you warm and stop the wetsuit from scratching you.
  2. Then your wetsuit. The wetsuit keeps you warm in the water if you capsize.
  3. If you have any, put on your sailing socks, these make your feet extra warm.
  4. After that your sailing shoes or boots. This stops your from cutting you feet on stones or rocks.
  5. Then your sailing gloves which keep your hands warm in the water.
  6. And at last your buoyancy aid or lifevest.

Those are the clothes you should wear.

Life-jackets

It is vital that life-jackets or buoyancy aids are worn while on the water. Life-jackets have special gasses inside them that blow them up automatically when you touch the water or when you pull a lever.

Buoyancy aids have a type of foam inside them that helps it to float. However, life-jackets can carry much more weight and will keep you floating for longer.
